Our services

Physiotherapy & wellness support

Evidence-based physiotherapy with friendly guidance for people of all ages. We blend manual therapy, progressive exercise plans, onsite workplace advice, kaumātua-friendly movement sessions, and restorative options tailored to every person we see.

Injury rehabilitation

Comprehensive programmes that restore strength and mobility after sprains, fractures, or surgery so you can return to work and whānau life safely.

Hands-on therapy

Joint mobilisation, soft-tissue release, and mirimiri that ease discomfort and calm busy muscles while supporting your natural healing process.

Tailored treatment plans

Goal-driven exercise progressions, education, and ergonomic advice that keep you moving confidently between in-clinic sessions.

Sports taping & support

Protective taping, bracing, and strapping that stabilises joints, boosts confidence, and keeps you performing at your best.

Dry needling

Targeted dry needling techniques that settle persistent trigger points and complement your broader physiotherapy plan.

Electrotherapy

Evidence-based electrotherapy options that reduce pain, assist muscle activation, and accelerate your recovery timeline.
