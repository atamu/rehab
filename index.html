PHYSIOTHERAPY SOLUTIONS

Manual Therapy

Hands-on joint mobilisation and soft tissue work that releases tension and restores easy movement.

Exercise Therapy

Personalised strength and mobility plans that build resilience between appointments.

Soft Tissue Treatment

Trigger point, myofascial, and massage techniques that ease muscle tightness and inflammation.

Acupuncture

Western medical acupuncture to calm pain pathways and support whole-body recovery.

Hydrotherapy

Pool-based rehab programmes that reduce load on joints while keeping fitness and confidence up.

Diagnostic & Specialist Referrals

Fast access to imaging, reporting, and trusted specialists when your rehab needs extra support.

Who We Help

We back tradies, parents, athletes, office crews, and kaumatua alike. Whether it’s a fall, a sports knock, or a weekend garden tweak, we’ll help you get back to what you love — without the pain.

What We Treat

From niggly necks and shoulders to sore hips, knees, shins, calves, ankles, and feet — if it’s a musculoskeletal injury, we’re on your side.

ACC Accredited & Southern Cross Easy Claim Provider

Wondering if you need to see a doctor first? Nah, you can come straight to us — we’ll sort your ACC claim in no time. We’re set up for Southern Cross Easy Claim as well, or we can walk you through the private consult route. Too easy.
